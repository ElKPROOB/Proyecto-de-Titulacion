### **Allearning** is a practical learning tool, created to facilitate the teaching of programming fundamentals.

### It was created in a context of pandemic by the Club of Algorithmia of the [*Center of Scientific and Technological Studies N°13*](https://www.cecyt13.ipn.mx/), from students for students.

![Captura de pantalla 2023-01-26 153910](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74994335/214972004-912dd997-47e1-4ca6-b061-8258bca315fc.png)


### It consists of different graphic lessons about basic programming topics, accompanied by some quizzes that encourage the student to test the acquired knowledge.

![Captura de pantalla 2023-01-26 154524](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74994335/214971917-25980c3b-f3ff-4077-a052-f940d184b22f.png)

![Captura de pantalla 2023-01-26 154901](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74994335/214972048-1451da95-e661-43c9-a49d-d89090661e40.png)


### Allearning's philosophy is to teach slowly but surely, with a distribution of knowledge in small segments. In order for students to practice what they learn step by step, and to be able to refer back to specific knowledge when they need it.

![Captura de pantalla 2023-01-26 155133](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74994335/214973568-50d99ebc-6779-44ff-aa85-16887c96227f.png)
